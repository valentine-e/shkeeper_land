How to Use SHKeeper?

SHKeeper equips you with extra efficiency in integrating crypto payment service with any other existing systems.

  • Integrate With Popular
    CMS and Plug-Ins

    SHKeeper is available for integration with any of the most popular CMSs, plugins, and billing systems. This is a completely digitalized and decentralized option. So you don't need to rely on third parties. The module for WordPress CMS and WooCommerce is available for free.

  • Integrate With Any
    E-commerce

    You can install SHKeeper yourself in your E-commerce project to accept cryptocurrency payments or contact us for help with installation, configuration, or maintenance. 0% commission and a high level of security.

  • Integrate With Your Own Code or Product

    SHKeeper is applicable to any product or code. It's an open-source crypto-processor and is available to everyone without the need for payment, and does not charge a transaction fee. You can also contact us if you need to fine-tune SHKeeper for your product.

  • Use as Your Own Wallet

    It is quite possible to use SHKeeper as a regular multi-currency crypto wallet. It can be accessed at any time using your private key. It allows you to back up the blockchain key, whereas you can also restore the key through your other crypto wallets.

Features

  • Direct Crypto Payment

    You receive direct payments from your customers without the intermediaries.

  • Customizable Rates

    The ability to independently adjust the rate of cryptocurrency for transactions.

  • Self-Setting Commission

    You can manage the commission rate if it's needed.

  • Overpayment Crediting

    You will get a notification when receiving a remittance larger than stated to your balance.

  • Partial Payments

    You can form for customers a partial payment for a product or service.

  • Auto-Withdrawals

    Auto withdrawals to a cold wallet according to the scheduler or accumulated amount.

  • Non-Custodial

    Only you have full control over your private key and wallet.

  • No KYC

    No need to pass identification and verification to use a payment processor.

  • Сustomizable Solution

    It's a fully customizable solution that stands for different business goals.

How SHKeeper Actually Works?

Why Should You Opt For SHKeeper?

Open-Source and Free of Charge

SHKeeper is free and open-source, self-hosted software. It allows you to receive payments directly to your software or hardware crypto wallet.

It's an excellent opportunity for developers to build things and integrations on top and around of SHKeeper. Extra competitive advantage never hurts. Thus you've got yourself your own personalized payment processor. Equipped with SHKeeper, technically, it is like having your own bank in your pocket.

Private and Secure

SHKeeper offers a direct way for store-owners to receive Bitcoin, Ethereum, LiteCoine, and other cryptocurrency payments, which are bound to improve the privacy of the customers significantly.

There are no subscriptions or transaction fees. No third-party or intermediary involvement significantly increases censorship resistance, privacy, and security. Last but not least, SHKeeper enables you to become a processor yourself.
